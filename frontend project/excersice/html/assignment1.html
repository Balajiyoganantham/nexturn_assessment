School Event To-Do List

Major Steps in Event Planning

  1. Book the Venue
  2. Send Invitations
  3. Prepare Event Schedule
  4. Arrange Food and Drinks
  5. Set Up Decorations
  6. Organize Volunteers
  7. Host the Event

Items Required for the Event

  • Decorations
  • Food
  • Drinks
  • Tables and Chairs
  • Sound System
  • Lighting

Key Roles and Responsibilities

Event Coordinator
Oversees the entire event and ensures everything runs smoothly.
Volunteer Manager
Responsible for organizing and assigning tasks to volunteers.
Logistics Coordinator
Manages the transportation, seating, and equipment needed for the event.
Food and Beverage Manager
Ensures food and drinks are arranged and served during the event.
